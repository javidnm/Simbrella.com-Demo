Simbrella launched FinTech partnership with First City Monument Bank (FCMB) in February 2020.

FCMB is a full-service commercial bank with more than 200 branches across Nigeria. The Bank has continued to distinguish itself through innovation and the delivery of exceptional services tailored to meet the individual and business aspirations of customers. Simbrella has designed several micro-lending solutions for more than 4 million registered customers of FCMB. These digital products help FCMB to provide financial services to the underbanked population of Nigeria. Tailoring our solutions according to the needs of the local people, we aim to provide sustainable development perspectives in the country.

Simbrella and FCMB look forward to broadening the horizons of their partnership with airtime lending solution. We will provide FCMB customers the opportunity to access mobile balance loans via their bank account.
